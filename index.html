Fully automated

The project automates stock market anlysis, investment decisions and buy/sell orders. It builds a model which identifies investment opportunities. When all criteria are met, the script places a real transaction order in an online trading platform.

MLOps

The system is consisted of complex data pipelines, with processes such as downloading the data from multiple sources, web scrapping, anomaly detection, quality assurence, feature generation, prediction, validation.

Simulation

The model performance is first verified with a reliable investment simulation on over 20 years of historical data. The model is frequently rebuilt to take the most current data into account. The final verification takes place in real-life investment.
